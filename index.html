WAR IS HERE

the details

Prepare your forces, battle to claim lands against enemies in this strategic war game and conquer all you encounter within your path.
Build your base and recruit units to fight, wether it be those who fight on the ground or from above - gain an advantage over your targets
with the ultimate level of planning.


Players are able to craft and place forts within the different biomes of the game, earning gold from placing mines which will
allow users to purchase resources to suit their needs. Attack the enemy by acquiring ground forces and attacking opposing land.


Keep in mind the different methods of collecting resources have specific biomes in which they are able to function. Conquering opponents may
not be as simple as you first anticipated.
